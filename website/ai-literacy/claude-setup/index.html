Getting your paid Claude account

The class is fully hands-on, so you'll need a paid Claude account (Claude Pro) to follow along live. Here's a short walkthrough — it takes about 5 minutes.

A quick screen recording of signing up for and upgrading to a paid Claude account.

  1. Go to claude.ai and create an account (or sign in).
  2. Open the plan/upgrade menu and choose the paid plan (Claude Pro).
  3. Complete checkout — you'll see "Pro" on your account once it's active.
  4. That's it! Bring this account (logged in) to the class on your laptop.

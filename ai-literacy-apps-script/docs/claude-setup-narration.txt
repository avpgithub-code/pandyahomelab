AI LITERACY CLASS — "Getting your paid Claude account" walkthrough
Narration script (~60 seconds)

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PLAIN NARRATION  (paste into a TTS tool, or read aloud)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
To join the AI Literacy Class hands-on, you'll need a paid Claude account. Here's how — it takes about a minute.

First, go to claude.ai and sign in, or create a free account first.

Then open the account menu in the bottom-left, where your name is, and click Upgrade. Choose the Pro plan.

Enter your payment details and confirm. You'll see "Pro" on your account once it's active.

That's it! Bring this account, logged in on your laptop, to class. See you there!

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
NOTES FOR RECORDING / TTS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Pronounce "claude.ai" as "claude dot A-I".
• Friendly, unhurried pace — aim for ~60–70 seconds total.
• Pause ~0.5s between each paragraph (matches the caption gaps).
• On-screen actions to match the voiceover:
    – Para 1: title / claude.ai homepage
    – Para 2: sign-in screen
    – Para 3: account menu → Upgrade → Pro plan
    – Para 4: checkout, then the "Pro" badge on the account
    – Para 5: end card ("See you in class!")
